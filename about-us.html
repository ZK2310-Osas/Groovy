Who are we?
We are a team of passionate students from Sunway University, combining our love for music and technology to create something extraordinary. Our music streaming platform began as an assignment for WEB1201 but quickly evolved into a vision to revolutionize how people experience music.
Our Goal
Our mission is to let everyone using the most user-friendly music website, seamless access to their favorite music and discovering new tunes effortlessly.
Our app connects listeners with an endless library of songs, personalized playlists, and a seamless listening experience. We aim to make music discovery fun, intuitive, and accessible to everyone."
